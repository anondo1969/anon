# Seventh Swedish Language Technology Conference, Stockholm, 7-9 November 2018

I worked as a volunteer in the [seventh Swedish Language Technology Conference, Stockholm, 7-9 November 2018](https://sltc2018.su.se/). I was very new in Stockholm at that time. The other volunteers, Johanna, Jacqueline, Adam, and Murathan were very helpful, cordial, and welcoming to me. I especially loved the snowy Stockholm University’s Frescati campus, the fashionable and mighty [Aula Magna](https://www.su.se/english/about-the-university/about-our-campus/book-conferences-and-meetings/aula-magna-1.508992), the cozy and nostalgic [Juristernas Hus](https://www.jurstud.com/juristernas-hus). I learned a lot from the conference chair Professor Mats Wirén, Department of Linguistics regarding how to efficiently organize such a big event. The banquet was held at [Stora Skuggans Värdshus](http://storaskuggansvardshus.se/). The destination path from the [Aula Magna](https://www.su.se/english/about-the-university/about-our-campus/book-conferences-and-meetings/aula-magna-1.508992) to there was quite a dream-like journey during that foggy night of 8 November. I loved and enjoyed it throughout the whole conference time.
