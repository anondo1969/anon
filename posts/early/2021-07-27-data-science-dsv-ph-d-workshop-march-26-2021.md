# Data Science @ DSV Ph.D. Workshop on March 26, 2021

The [Data Science Research Group](https://datascience.dsv.su.se/) at the Department of Computer and Systems Sciences (DSV) of Stockholm University organized a [Ph.D. Workshop](https://datascience.dsv.su.se/dsworkshop2021.html) where Ph.D. students presented their ongoing research. Mykola Pechenizkiy (Eindhoven University of Technology, The Netherlands) was the keynote speaker on the topic 'On Explainable Machine Learning'. Along with Uno Fors, Jelena Zdravkovic, and the [Data Science Research Group](https://datascience.dsv.su.se/) from DSV, the respected external participants were as follows,

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Allan Tucker | Brunel Univ. |
| Myra Spiliopoulou | Univ. Magdeburg |
| Michael Fisher | Univ. Manchester |
| Joakim Dillner | Karolinska Institute |
| NIklas Noren | World Health Organization |
| Aris Gionis | KTH |

I presented my ongoing research entitled '[Internet of medical things with distributed machine learning](https://stockholmuniversity.app.box.com/s/bi1p6d8m8y0y7nthh599jgi4vfqndn7k/file/796414801771).' I learned a lot from the other presenters. The comments and feedbacks regarding my presentation were quite insightful. Overall it was a thoroughly enjoyable experience. I cordially thank the [Data Science Research Group](https://datascience.dsv.su.se/) for organizing this event which provides me such a nice opportunity to learn from the very bests.
